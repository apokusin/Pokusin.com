Charge: Mobile Carrier

Referral program

Charge has a pretty sweet referral program. I worked to design and market the referral program through multiple channels.

First, I designed and developed a mini-landing page which our marketing campaigns would link back to.

I made sure to include the proper social media tags so that our posts would look nice in places like Twitter and Facebook (although generic meta tags are included as well).

I also included a version of the banner on our primary landing page.

The final mention of referrals is in a prominent banner at the top of the Charge dashboard. This banner includes a referral code and buttons to share it.
